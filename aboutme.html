Hi, I'm Sean.

I'm a third-year Computer Science student currently enrolled at the University of Ottawa in Canada. Ever since I first learned to code in High School, I've been passionate about software development. I have experience with numerous programming languages and technologies, and am always eager to learn new skills!

I've been programming for about six years and have worked on numerous projects over that time. Some of my work includes the Lights Out! Android game, which has been downloaded over a hundred times on Google Play, as well as a variety of web and mobile applications. More information about these projects can be found on my Projects page!

I'm fluently bilingual, having attained my DELF B2 certification from the Ministry of Education of France, as well as passing the Second Language Certification Test at the University of Ottawa. Bilingualism is important to me, I've helped promote the French language for clubs at uOttawa, such as the Google Developer Student Club, as well as promoting uOttawa's French Immersion program.

Beyond my academic and professional life, I enjoy travelling and seeing new places. I'm a fairly average Chess player and enjoy playing Blitz or Rapid games on Lichess. I learned how to rollerblade during the pandemic, although I'm still not much good at it. I also enjoy writing on Medium about topics that interest me at any given time. I'm a Blue Jays fan, and am probably one of the only Canadians that doesn't enjoy hockey much. I'm also one of the moderators for /r/geegees, uOttawa's official subreddit!

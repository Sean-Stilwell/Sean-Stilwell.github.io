About Me!

Hi! My name is Sean Stilwell. I'm a third-year Computer Science student at the University of Ottawa with experience in a variety of languages and technologies and several years of development experience.
I've been programming for around five years and have built numerous projects over that time. Some examples of my work are the Lights Out! mobile game, which has been downloaded several hundred times, as well as developing a mobile application for a fictional clinic and a competition management website. More information about these projects (and others!) can be found on my Projects page.
I'm fluently bilingual, having attained my DELF B2 certification from the Ministry of Education of France, as well as passing the Second Language Certification Test at the University of Ottawa. I previously worked as the Marketing and Communications lead for the Google Developer Student Club at uOttawa.
I also write articles on Medium about any topics that interest me.

About Me!

Hi! My name is Sean Stilwell. I'm a third-year Computer Science student at the University of Ottawa with experience in a variety of languages and technologies, and several years of development experience.
I've been programming for about 5 years, and I'm proficient in a variety of languages. I have experience creating applications in C#, Java, and Python, and have also learned concurrent, logical, and functional programming using Golang, Prolog, and Racket respectively. I'm also experienced with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP for web design, and SQL-based languages for databases.
I'm fluently bilingual, having attained my DELF B2 certification from the Ministry of Education of France, as well as passing the Second Language Certification Test at the University of Ottawa. I'm passionate about promoting bilingualism, especially for Canadian youth!

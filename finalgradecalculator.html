How does the math work?

For most classes, the final grade is a sum of the grades you've earned multiplied by their weight. For example, where homework is worth 30%, a midterm 25%, and a final exam 45%, the final grade would be 0.3 * Homework Average + 0.25 * Midterm Mark + 0.45 * Exam Mark.
Using simple algebra, you can rearrange this formula to solve for the mark you need on the final to get a mark you want. The formula for that is Exam Mark = (Target Mark - (Current Mark * (1 - Final Weight))) / Final Weight

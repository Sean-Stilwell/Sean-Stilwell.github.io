About Me

I am a 4th-year student at the University of Ottawa, currently completing an Honours Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and an accelerated Master of Computer Science. I have two years of work experience in the Government of Canada performing a variety of roles. 

My research focus is on Natural Language Processing for legal documents. I'm highly experienced with Python, having performed geospatial, bioinformatics, NLP, and other assorted AI/ML tasks with it. I also enjoy web development and have experience with React, Node.js, and Ruby on Rails. I have back-end experience with Firebase and PostgreSQL. I'm also comfortable with C, C++, C#, Kotlin, Swift, and Go.

I'm fluently bilingual in English and French. I am certified at the B2 level by the Ministry of Education of France, and attained the uOttawa's Second Language Certification for French.
